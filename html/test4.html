Happiness Test

5 minutes

1. I can find the good in even the most disagreeable people.
2. I can find the good in even the most disagreeable people.
3. I can find something positive in even the most difficult situations.
4. I actively keep in touch with friends and family.
5. When I have a difficult problem, I try to look at it from different angles in order to come up with a solution.
6. When I'm feeling down, I remind myself to focus on the good things in my life instead of the bad.
7. When I find myself overwhelmed with stress, I shut down completely.
8. If I ever need help, I believe that my friends and/or family will be there for me.
9. I'm better off looking after myself because I can't rely on others to do so.
10. No matter what life throws at me, I believe that I can deal with it.
